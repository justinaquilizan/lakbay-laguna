About this destination

Visit the historic Diocesan Shrine & Parish of St. John the Baptist, a significant religious landmark in Calamba. This beautiful church holds great historical and cultural importance as it was where Dr. Jose Rizal was baptized and where his family regularly attended mass.

The church features stunning Spanish colonial architecture, intricate religious artwork, and a peaceful atmosphere perfect for reflection and prayer. The surrounding plaza and historical markers add to the site's cultural significance and make it a must-visit destination for those interested in Philippine history and religious heritage.
