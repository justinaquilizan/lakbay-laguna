Mt. Makiling

4.8 (156 reviews) • Calamba, Laguna

About this destination

Embark on a refreshing trek through the lush forests of Mt. Makiling, a dormant volcano rich in biodiversity, myth, and natural beauty. Whether you're a casual hiker or a nature enthusiast, this journey offers a peaceful escape and a chance to connect with the environment.

Your adventure starts with a guided hike along well-maintained trails that lead you through dense forest, serene streams, and scenic viewpoints. Along the way, you might encounter rare flora and fauna and hear the legendary tales of Maria Makiling, the mountain's mythical guardian.
