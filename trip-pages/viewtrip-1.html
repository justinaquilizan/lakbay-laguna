Pagsanjan Falls Adventure

4.8 (128 reviews) • Pagsanjan, Laguna

About this destination

Experience the thrill of shooting the rapids through a spectacular gorge and marvel at the majestic Pagsanjan Falls. This adventure combines nature, excitement, and Filipino hospitality in one unforgettable journey.

Your journey begins with a scenic boat ride upstream, navigating through 14 rapids with skilled boatmen. The climax of the trip is reaching the magnificent falls, where you can take a bamboo raft ride behind the cascading water.
