About this destination

Step into history at the Bahay ni Rizal, the ancestral home of the Philippines' national hero, Dr. Jose Rizal. This meticulously preserved Spanish colonial house offers a fascinating glimpse into the life and times of one of the country's most influential figures.

The museum houses original furniture, artifacts, and memorabilia from Rizal's life, including his personal belongings and family heirlooms. The surrounding garden and the replica of the original house provide an immersive experience into the environment that shaped the young Rizal.
