Banga Calamba City

4.7 (245 reviews) • Calamba, Laguna

About this destination

Experience the rich cultural heritage of Calamba at Banga, a traditional Filipino restaurant that offers an authentic taste of local cuisine and history. The restaurant is housed in a beautifully restored ancestral house that showcases traditional Filipino architecture and design.

Banga is known for its delicious Filipino dishes, particularly their specialty rice meals served in traditional clay pots (banga). The restaurant's ambiance, combined with its historical significance and excellent food, makes it a must-visit destination for those looking to experience authentic Filipino culture and cuisine.
